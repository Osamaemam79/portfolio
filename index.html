My Campaigns

TBK North Coast

As part of my role as a Marketing Specialist, I was actively involved in the successful execution of a comprehensive campaign for El Madina El Menwara, a prominent dairy brand. The campaign focused on promoting their latest product, Tres Leches, which featured four enticing flavors: chocolate, caramel, cream, and pistachio.

To capture the essence of summer and appeal to our target audience, we meticulously planned and conducted a captivating photo session in Sahel, The vibrant and visually appealing images showcased the Tres Leches product in an enticing manner, creating a strong connection with consumers.

In addition to the outdoor photo session, we strategically utilized in-store locations to further highlight the product. We collaborated with professional photographers and videographers to create engaging reels and captivating visuals that showcased the various flavors of Tres Leches. Voice-overs were incorporated to enhance the overall impact and communicate the unique features and benefits of the product effectively.

Recognizing the influence of social media and the power of peer recommendations, we partnered with three prominent food bloggers who resonated strongly with the target youth audience. These bloggers provided authentic and enthusiastic reviews of the Tres Leches product, amplifying its appeal and generating buzz among their followers.

By combining compelling visuals, strategic location choices, and leveraging the influence of trusted influencers, our campaign successfully targeted the youth demographic and effectively promoted the new Tres Leches flavors for El Madina El Menwara. The campaign's results demonstrated increased brand awareness, heightened engagement, and ultimately, a positive impact on sales.

TBK North Coast

For TBK latest campaign, I spearheaded a dynamic campaign for TBK (The Balkan Kitchen), an esteemed fine dining restaurant specializing in Balkan cuisine and prime cuts. The campaign aimed to promote the opening of their new summer branch in Sahel, offering guests a unique and delightful dining experience.

One of the main highlights of the campaign revolved around the enchanting atmosphere that awaited patrons at the restaurant. Nestled along the beachfront, the new branch boasted breathtaking views of the sea, creating an idyllic setting for enjoying grilled delicacies and prime cuts. We wanted to convey this captivating ambiance to our target audience, enticing them with the promise of an unforgettable dining experience. ...

To capture the essence of this extraordinary setting, we meticulously planned and executed a captivating photo session. The photos showcased the picturesque beach and sea views, while also highlighting the delectable dishes and prime cuts that TBK is renowned for. These visually appealing images were then incorporated into engaging reels, accompanied by a compelling voice-over that accentuated the unique features and ambiance of the restaurant.

In order to amplify the reach and impact of the campaign, we collaborated with four influential food bloggers who have a strong following. These renowned bloggers were invited to experience TBK's new summer branch firsthand and share their personal experiences with their dedicated audience. Their reviews emphasized the exceptional quality of the food, the mesmerizing views, and the innovative concept of allowing guests to create and grill their own burgers at the table, adding an interactive and engaging element to the dining experience.

Through the combination of captivating visuals, strategic influencer partnerships, and the promotion of the restaurant's unique offerings, our campaign successfully generated buzz and anticipation for TBK's new summer branch. It effectively conveyed the allure of the beachside atmosphere and highlighted the exceptional dining experience that awaited patrons.

Pizza King Fold

Latest for pizza king, I led an exciting campaign for Pizza King, a renowned pizza chain, to introduce their latest innovation: the Pizza Fold. Inspired by the concept of pizza melts, we took it to the next level by creating a larger, more indulgent version with a wide variety of flavors. The Pizza Fold revolutionized the way individuals could enjoy a satisfying and complete meal in a convenient and handheld format.

To showcase the appeal and versatility of the Pizza Fold, we organized a captivating photo session within Pizza King's stores. The photos captured the mouthwatering visuals of the folded pizzas, highlighting their generous fillings, delectable toppings, and perfectly melted cheese. These enticing images were then utilized in various promotional materials, both online and offline, to create a strong visual impact and generate interest among potential customers.

Recognizing the influential power of food bloggers and their ability to sway consumer opinions, we collaborated with four popular Foodbloggers who possess a strong following. These bloggers were invited to experience the Pizza Fold firsthand and share their authentic and positive reviews with their dedicated audience. Their testimonials highlighted the unique flavors, the substantial portion sizes, and the convenience of the Pizza Fold, positioning it as a satisfying alternative to ordering a traditional pizza.

By combining visually appealing imagery, strategic influencer collaborations, and emphasizing the Pizza Fold as a complete meal in itself, our campaign successfully generated excitement and curiosity among pizza lovers. It effectively conveyed the value proposition of the Pizza Fold as a convenient, flavorful, and satisfying option for individuals seeking a delicious and fulfilling meal.
